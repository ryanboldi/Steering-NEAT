What is steering evolution?
Well, it is a project I created to see if i could train neural networks to steer around obsticles to reach a final goal.

What are the creatures?
I'm glad you asked. Balls are creatures that have a brain with 4 inputs, and 2 outputs:

The 4 inputs are:

  • Sight from left eye
  • Sight from middle eye
  • Sight from right eye
  • Angle to goal
The 2 outputs are:
  • Probability to turn left
  • Probability to turn right
If either of the probabilities are above 0.5, the creature would turn in that respective direction.
